About me

I am an Iranian aerosol scientist and researcher, currently engaged as a postdoctoral fellow in mechanical engineering at the University of Alberta. I received my Ph.D. from the University of Alberta in 2022 under the supervision of Prof. Jason Olfert. In my doctoral studies, I was involved in developing a novel aerosol instrument technique to characterize black carbon particulates for which I received a number of prestigious awards and scholarships, including Alberta Innovates Graduate Scholarships (AIGS) and Alberta Graduate Excellence Scholarship (AGES).
